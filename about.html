About Us

The hands and minds behind the project

Our Vision

Faculty of Arts
Media Department

We are a collective of students, researchers, and storytellers united by a singular purpose: to resurrect the living soul of Ancient Egypt through the lens of modern media. Our project is not merely an academic exercise — it is an act of cultural devotion.

Through immersive digital experiences, scholarly research, and visual artistry, we bridge the abyss of five millennia, bringing the grandeur of the pharaohs into the consciousness of a global audience that has never stood before a temple or held a papyrus scroll.

Every artefact we study, every inscription we decode, every monument we document is a thread in an unbroken tapestry connecting our present civilisation to its most magnificent ancestor.
